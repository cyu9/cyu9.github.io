Currently an embedded software engineer at Stellartech. Previously worked as a systems engineer and software test engineer at various medical device companies (Siemens Healthcare, Philips Volcano, Beckman Coulter).

Currently pursuing a Doctor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering at Columbia University (supervised by Professor Mingoo Seok and his VLSI Lab). My research interests are neuromorphic and bio-inspired computing. Previously, I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech and my B.S. in Biomedical Engineering from Purdue University.

I love playing and training for sports - currently dedicated to hockey and olympic lifting, trying new teas, and browsing reddit.

The projects page is a nice place to see some of my work, but you can also view my resume.

Born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri; worked in Sacramento, CA after undergrad; currently in the San Francisco Bay Area.
